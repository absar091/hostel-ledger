# Typography Update - Complete ✅

## Summary
All major app pages (excluding auth pages as requested) now have consistent iPhone-style typography applied.

## Typography Standards Applied

### Font Weights
- **Headers/Titles**: `font-black` (900 weight) with `tracking-tight`
- **Section Labels**: `font-black uppercase tracking-widest text-xs`
- **Body Text**: `font-bold` (700 weight)
- **Amounts/Numbers**: `font-black tracking-tight tabular-nums`
- **Subtle Text**: `font-semibold` or `font-medium`
- **Card Titles**: `font-black tracking-tight`

### Color Scheme
- Primary: `#4a6850` (greenish-gray)
- Text: `text-gray-900` for main content
- Secondary: `text-[#4a6850]/80` for supporting text
- Amounts: Color-coded (red for expenses, green/primary for income)
