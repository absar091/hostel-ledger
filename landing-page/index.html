Built for Students, By Students

Hostel Ledger was created to solve the everyday problem of managing shared expenses in hostels and shared accommodations. We understand the awkwardness of asking for money and the confusion of who paid for what. That's why we built a simple, transparent, and free solution that makes splitting expenses effortless.

Whether you're sharing a room, an apartment, or just splitting bills with friends, Hostel Ledger keeps everyone on the same page and eliminates money-related stress.
